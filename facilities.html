Located at 685 Fox Trail in Opelika, Alabama, our facility sits on over 1 million square feet of land with a 246,350 square foot manufacturing building. The site was purpose-selected for its strategic proximity to both Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama in Montgomery and Kia Georgia in West Point.

Injection Molding

Our injection molding operation features a fleet of machines that were all newly invested starting in 2021 — no legacy equipment. In April 2025, three machines were upgraded to next-generation LS injection molding systems, further enhancing our precision and cycle efficiency.

Our molding capabilities support the full range of interior component sizes, from small trim pieces to large console housings, with tight tolerances required for OEM assembly fit.

Mold Shop

Our in-house mold shop provides rapid tooling maintenance, modifications, and repairs. This capability minimizes downtime and ensures we can respond quickly to engineering changes or production demands without relying on external vendors.
