Product Categories

Quality Commitment

Every component we produce is manufactured under our IATF 16949 certified quality management system. With a 2025 customer defect rate of just 1.96 PPM, we consistently deliver parts that exceed OEM expectations.

Our quality process encompasses incoming material inspection, in-process monitoring, and final product verification — ensuring traceability and conformance at every stage.
