About · AGS America Inc.

From distributor
to manufacturer.

A Tier 2 automotive manufacturer in Opelika, Alabama, delivering precision interior and exterior trim to Hyundai and Kia assembly plants across the Southeast — molded and assembled under one roof since 2020.

Our Story

The road so far.

01 / Heritage
  1. 2018

    Distribution begins

    Opened a distribution center in Opelika, Alabama — the first AGS footprint in the U.S. automotive supply chain, in the heart of the Southeast auto corridor.

  2. 2020

    Manufacturing entity established

    AGS America Inc. was established as a separate legal entity, entering automotive manufacturing with a dedicated production facility — molding brought fully in-house.

  3. 2021

    All-new equipment

    Invested in an entirely new fleet of injection molding machines — no legacy equipment — building a state-of-the-art operation from the ground up.

  4. 2023

    $79M revenue · 270% growth

    Reached $79M in annual sales — a 270% increase — driven by the transfer of console production lines for multiple Hyundai and Kia programs.

  5. 2025

    Next-gen presses · 2.03 PPM

    Replaced three machines with next-generation LS injection systems and achieved a 2.03 PPM customer defect rate — world-class quality, measured at the OEM line.

What We Do

Molded, assembled,
delivered.

We specialize in automotive interior and exterior components through injection molding and assembly. As a dedicated Tier 2 supplier, we focus exclusively on production excellence — delivering consoles, trim panels, and cockpit modules that meet the exacting standards of our OEM customers.

Our location in Opelika places us within 72 miles of Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA) in Montgomery and 26 miles of Kia Georgia (KaGA) in West Point — enabling reliable just-in-time delivery to both assembly plants.
