About Us

Driving Automotive
Excellence Forward

AGS America Inc is a Tier 2 automotive manufacturer based in Opelika, Alabama, delivering precision interior components to Hyundai and Kia assembly plants across the Southeast.

Our Story

2018
Opened a distribution center in Opelika, Alabama, establishing our initial presence in the U.S. automotive supply chain.
2020
AGS America Inc was established as a separate legal entity, entering the automotive manufacturing business with a dedicated production facility.
2021
Invested in all-new injection molding equipment, building a state-of-the-art manufacturing operation from the ground up.
2023
Achieved $79M in annual sales, a 270% increase driven by the transfer of console production lines for multiple Hyundai and Kia programs.
2025
Replaced 3 injection machines with next-generation LS equipment. Achieved 1.96 PPM customer defect rate, demonstrating world-class quality.

What We Do

We specialize in the manufacturing of automotive interior components through injection molding and assembly operations. As a dedicated Tier 2 supplier, we focus exclusively on production excellence — delivering consoles, trim panels, and cockpit modules that meet the exacting standards of our OEM customers.

Our strategic location in Opelika places us within 72 miles of Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA) in Montgomery and just 25 miles from Kia Georgia (KaGA) in West Point, enabling reliable just-in-time delivery to both assembly plants.
